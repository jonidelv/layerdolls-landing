Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2025

Layerdolls ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our bookmark manager service and browser extension.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our service:

  • Email address: Used for account creation and authentication (passwordless login)
  • Bookmarks: URLs, titles, and page content you choose to save
  • Collections: Folders and organization structure you create

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Layerdolls service
  • Send authentication emails for login
  • Enable full-text search across your saved bookmarks
  • Sync your bookmarks between devices

Browser Extension Permissions

The Layerdolls Chrome extension requests the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To save the current page as a bookmark when you click the extension
  • storage: To store your authentication token locally
  • tabs: To read the URL and title of the current tab for saving bookmarks

The extension only accesses page information when you actively choose to save a bookmark. We do not track your browsing history or collect data from pages you don't save.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on servers hosted in the European Union
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes

Data Retention

Your bookmarks and account data are retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: To generate summaries of saved page content (content is processed but not stored by OpenAI)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your bookmarks
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request information about how your data is used

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
